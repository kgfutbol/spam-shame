Spam Shame

How to filter certain "dot emails"

  1. Go to Gmail
  2. Click the settings gear
  3. Click "See all settings"
  4. Go to Labels
  5. Create a new label called "spam_shame"
  6. Select the option to make things labelled "spam_shame" are hidden in message list
  7. Go to "Filters and Blocked Addresses"
  8. Click "Create a new filter"
  9. Click "Create a new filter"
  10. Click "Create a new filter"
  11. Enter the "dot email" in the To field
  12. Select to assign the created label to any matching emails
